# Anything Daiquiri
If you use a blender, we cannot be friends.

## Basic Daiquiri
    1 oz.  Light Rum
    1 oz.  Dark Rum
    3/4 oz. Lime Juice
    3/4 oz. Simple Syrup

Shake over ice.

### Variations

## Cardamom Daiquiri
    2 oz. Plantation Three Star Rum
    1 oz. Lime Juice
    1 oz. Cardamom Syrup
    1/2 oz. Pierre Ferrand Dry Curacao
    1 dash Orange Bitters

## Hemingway Daiquiri
    2 oz. Light Rum
    1/2 oz. Maraschino
    3/4 oz. Lime Juice
    1/2 oz. Grapefruit Juice
